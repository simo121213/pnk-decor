Our Services

“Something different” means something different to every client. That’s why our forward-thinking designers, florists, producers, and strategists start with a blank canvas and partner with you or your event planner to produce a truly memorable event.

We provide quality services in the wedding and event industry including:

Wedding Styling

Elegant themes tailored to your big day.

Engagement Theme

Romantic concepts for special proposals.

Ruracio/Dowry Ceremonies

Celebrate love the traditional way with beautifully styled Ruracio events, blending rich Kikuyu heritage with elegant modern décor.

Themed House Parties

Balloon garlands, custom signage, lighting, and stylish lounge areas.

Elegant Tent & Seating Décor

Transform any space into a beautiful venue with our expertly styled tents and custom seating arrangements — perfect for weddings, parties, and all special occasions.

Church Décor

We specialize in elegant and spiritual church décor for weddings, baptisms, and special ceremonies. From aisle styling to altar arrangements, we transform sacred spaces into heavenly scenes with flowers, fabrics, and lighting tailored to your theme.

Birthday Parties

We create vibrant and fun birthday setups for kids and adults. Balloons, themed props, and personalized touches make your celebration unforgettable.

Baby Showers

Celebrate the joy of new beginnings with our charming baby shower décor. Soft colors, cute themes, and elegant setups perfect for welcoming your bundle of joy.
